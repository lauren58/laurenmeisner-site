About.

Lauren Meisner is a founder, consumer-culture strategist, and leading voice on the attention economy based in Sydney.

She is the Founder & Director of Centennial World, an award-winning Gen Z media network she bootstrapped to 20 million reach per month, and the founder of SponStudio, a platform rethinking how podcasts get funded.

Today she advises brands and marketers on consumer culture, audience behaviour, and turning cultural insight into commercial strategy — how businesses can earn attention rather than chase it.

In 2019 she spotted a gap for a youth publisher covering internet culture and the creator economy with the seriousness those subjects deserved, and built Centennial World to fill it. She began her career as a writer in digital publishing, with bylines in ELLE Australia and Harper's BAZAAR.
