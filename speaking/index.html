Speaking.

Lauren speaks on the attention economy and new media. Her expertise lies in how young audiences actually pay attention online, and what brands and media have to do to earn it. She keynotes, moderates and joins panels across founder festivals, global tech summits and industry events, drawing on what she's learned building Centennial World from the ground up.
